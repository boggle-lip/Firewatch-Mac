## 🧠 What is Firewatch for Mac?

Firewatch is a story-driven adventure game where players take on the role of Henry, a fire lookout stationed in a remote forest. The game emphasizes dialogue, exploration, and uncovering a mysterious storyline through radio communication with your supervisor.

It is designed to:

* Explore a vast wilderness environment
* Experience a strong narrative story
* Use radio-based dialogue choices
* Uncover a mystery in the forest
* Make emotional and story-driven decisions
* Focus on atmosphere and exploration
